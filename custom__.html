Stonetone Guitars is a custom guitar shop based in Conway, South Carolina, specializing in handcrafted electric guitars, upgrades, setups, and overhauls. Born from a love of vintage tone and punk grit, Stonetone offers fully custom builds using high-quality components, personalized finishes, and pro-level craftsmanship. Whether you're chasing classic Strat tones, building your dream Les Paul-style rig, or reviving a worn-out favorite, Stonetone delivers instruments built to rip.

Our services include custom guitar builds, full setups, fret leveling, pickup swaps, hardware upgrades, rewiring, and more. Every Stonetone guitar is tested, tuned, and stage-ready. We proudly serve the Grand Strand area, Myrtle Beach, and beyond, and we’re home to Pickles the Tone Gremlin , our rebellious mascot and spirit guide for all things loud.
